### Step 2: Create Account or Login

#### For Students (First Time):
1. Click **"Start Learning"** button
2. Click **"Sign up here"** 
3. Fill in the form:
   - Full Name
   - Student ID

   - Confirm Password

5. You'll be redirected to your Dashboard

#### For Admin Access:
1. Click **"Start Learning"** button
2. Enter:
   - Username: **admin**
   - Password: **admin123**
3. Click **"Login"**
4. You'll see your Dashboard with **"Admin Panel"** button

### Step 3: Explore Features

#### As Student:
- View your profile information
- See your Student ID and Class Level
- Access Virtual Labs, Study Materials, and Practice Tests
- Click **"Logout"** when done

#### As Admin:
- Click **"Admin Panel"** button (purple, in top right)
- View all registered users
- Monitor login sessions from all devices
- See statistics and analytics
- Switch between "Registered Users" and "Login Sessions" tabs
